About the Artist

"Multiple layers define depth and movement."

Working in oil, acrylic, and mixed media on canvas, Porter's abstract expressionist paintings trace the tension between wild landscape and interior memory. Drawing from the mountains of Colorado and the Gulf Coast shores of her Osprey home, her layered canvases are held in private collections across the US and Mexico.
