About the Artist

Capturing the light in every child's eyes.

Lorena Duarte is a Miami-based portrait photographer specializing in children and family photography. With over a decade of experience, her work is defined by natural light, genuine emotion, and an instinctive gift for putting even the most camera-shy subjects at ease.

Based in Miami, FL 33193, Lorena works on location throughout South Florida — bringing her studio sensibility to beaches, parks, gardens, and family homes alike.
