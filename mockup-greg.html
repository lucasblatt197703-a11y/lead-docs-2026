About

Eight years.
Every moment.

Greg Rubin has photographed and filmed weddings across South Florida for over eight years — from intimate backyard ceremonies to ballroom celebrations in Broward, Miami, and Palm Beach counties.

A native New Yorker who found his home in South Florida, Greg works with a documentary instinct: quiet observation, real light, and a camera that disappears into the room.

"The best wedding photos aren't posed. They happen in the five seconds before and after the pose."
