The person behind the lens

Based in Central Florida, Kassidy Griffin has spent six years crafting portraits and wedding photographs that capture people at their most genuine. Relocating from Illinois in 2020, she built a new client base from scratch — which speaks to the quality of the work. Her approach is unhurried, natural-light-first, and rooted in making subjects feel at ease before the shutter clicks.
