About

Two Decades of Telling
Love Stories

"I've always believed the best photos aren't posed — they're caught."

Established in 2004, Mariani Photography has documented over two decades of South Florida love stories — from intimate elopements in Key West to grand celebrations at Vizcaya. Amy's approach blends photojournalistic instinct with a fine-art eye, creating images that are cinematic without being contrived.

Bilingual in English and Spanish, she brings ease and genuine warmth to every family and couple she works with — a quality her clients note again and again.
