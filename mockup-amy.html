About Amy

Amy Xie has been documenting proposals and weddings since 2016, working across New York City and Southwest Florida. Known for capturing the unrehearsed reaction — the split-second of disbelief, the tears before the yes — her work is built on preparation and invisibility. Based in Tampa, available anywhere.
