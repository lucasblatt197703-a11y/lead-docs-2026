03 · The painter

Drawn to what
the camera misses

"Realism isn't about copying what you see — it's about painting what you feel when you look."

Based in Tampa, Florida, Lorena works primarily in oil on canvas and linen, with a parallel practice in graphite for portraiture and figure studies. Her hyperrealist approach draws on years of careful observation — an insistence on getting the light exactly right, on finding the expression that lives just beneath the surface of a face.

Commissions form the core of the studio practice. Each piece is approached as a conversation between subject and painter — a collaboration that takes months but lasts a lifetime.
