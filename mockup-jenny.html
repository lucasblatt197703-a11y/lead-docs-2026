About

The Person Behind the Lens

"A great headshot doesn't just capture a face — it captures the impression you want to leave."

Jenny Lopez is a professional headshot photographer based in Orlando, FL. Operating out of a dedicated studio at Sunport Drive, Jenny has spent five years helping executives, actors, and entrepreneurs put their best face forward in a competitive world.

Her work has been recognized in Forbes, New York Times, LA Weekly, and Orlando Voyager. Whether it's a corporate team shoot or a one-on-one actor portfolio session, Jenny's approach is calm, efficient, and — above all — results-driven.
