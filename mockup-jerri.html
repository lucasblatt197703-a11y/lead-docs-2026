The Artist

Jerri Phillips

"Art is the intersection of skill and soul — every commission tells a story worth painting."

Working primarily in oil, acrylic, and mixed media, Jerri Phillips has built a practice rooted in Florida's coastal culture and community. Based in Ellenton, her studio work spans intimate portrait commissions, sweeping murals, and historically significant Florida subjects — including the 2016 Heritage Day U.S. Postal cancellation stamp design for the Palmetto Historical Park.

A fixture in the Manatee County arts community, Phillips has shown work at local galleries and exhibitions, and maintains an active commission practice open to clients throughout Florida and beyond.
