Portrait Commissions

Oil on Canvas  ·  People, Pets & Children

Each portrait is painted in traditional oil on canvas — the same medium that has preserved likenesses for five centuries. Working from reference photographs, Michele creates finished works that carry the weight and permanence of Old Master originals. Commissions are available for individuals, families, children, and beloved pets throughout South Florida and beyond.

"The detail in a proper oil portrait cannot be reproduced by photography. It is a different kind of truth — one that endures."
— Michele Powell Beaujardin

Working primarily in oil on canvas, Michele Powell Beaujardin's portraits draw from the craft traditions of the Old Masters — precise observation, layered glazes, and an abiding attention to the humanity in her subjects. Her mural work, undertaken in collaboration with Dominique DuBois under the Enchanted Windows name, brings the same illusionistic discipline to architectural scale.
