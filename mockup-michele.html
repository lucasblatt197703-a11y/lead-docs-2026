Commission Work

Portrait Commissions

Oil on Canvas · Custom Work · People, Pets, Children

Each portrait is painted in traditional oil on canvas — the same medium that has preserved likenesses for five centuries. Working from reference photographs or live sessions, Michele creates finished works that carry the weight and permanence of period originals. Commissions are available for individuals, families, children, and beloved pets.

Commission inquiries: mbeaujardin@bellsouth.net
