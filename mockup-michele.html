The Artist

"Oil on canvas has a warmth no other medium can replicate — it breathes, it ages, it becomes part of the home."

Michele Powell Beaujardin is a Miami-based oil painter with over three decades of practice in portraiture, still life, and trompe l'oeil mural work. Operating under two studios — Beaujardin Fine Art and Enchanted Windows — she accepts commissions ranging from intimate family portraits to large-scale architectural murals for private residences and commercial spaces throughout South Florida.
