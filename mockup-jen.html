About the Photographer

Every woman deserves to see herself as art.

Jen Candela is a luxury high-end portrait photographer based out of Hernando County, Florida, specializing in custom glamour and boudoir portraiture. Serving clients across Spring Hill, Weeki Wachee, Brooksville, and Central Florida, she creates intimate, empowering experiences that go far beyond a photo session.

"You deserve to see yourself the way the people who love you see you."

Every session is tailored from start to finish — from curating the wardrobe and styling direction to the final portrait collection. Jen's signature "Girls Night In" events bring the full luxury studio experience to groups, complete with hair, makeup, and custom glamour photography.
