About
Delsie

"Every family deserves images they'll reach for in thirty years."

Delsie Figueroa documents the moments that matter most — the first breath, the first look, the quiet in-between. Based in Tampa's Carrollwood neighborhood, she brings a warm, unhurried approach to newborn, wedding, and portrait photography across the Tampa Bay area. Her work is defined by natural light, genuine emotion, and an instinct for the details families will want to remember.
