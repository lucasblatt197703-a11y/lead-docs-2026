From the Streets

From Custom Paint
to Canvas

Roly Fernandez spent years transforming vehicles in Miami's custom car scene — where precision, color theory, and visual impact aren't academic concepts, they're survival skills. That same discipline now drives his canvas work: bold abstracts that carry the weight of metal, speed, and Miami heat.

His work has been featured on Artsy and exhibited internationally under the title "Miami's King of Kustoms," running November 2024 through November 2025. Every painting is an original — no prints, no editions.
