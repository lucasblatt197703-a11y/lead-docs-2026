Born in Michigan, raised across four countries, Derek Latta has spent over two decades mastering the craft of commercial photography from his base in Miami Beach. His work spans headshots, fitness, resort, and lifestyle — united by a single principle.

"If you master lighting, composition and people skills, you can shoot anything and make it look great."

His images have run in national advertising campaigns, TV productions including the David Letterman Show and Doctor Oz, and across buses, billboards, and media throughout South Florida.
