About Savannah

"Every baby deserves to have their first days remembered beautifully."

Based in Palm Coast, Florida, Savannah Payne has spent 14 years capturing the moments that matter most — the first breath, the first hold, the quiet mornings of new parenthood. Working from her 1,000-square-foot studio and traveling throughout the Jacksonville to Daytona corridor, her work is guided by patience, warmth, and an obsession with beautiful light.

What began as wanting to document her own children's early years became a calling to help families hold onto theirs. Today, Savannah offers studio and on-location sessions across Flagler County and beyond — every image made with care, skill, and heart.
