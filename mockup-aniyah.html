About

Aniyah Ross is the founder of AJR Photography and BeYourself Studios, a creative portrait studio in Orlando's metro area. With over 27,000 followers and a client list that spans graduation portraits to school yearbooks to fashion editorials, her work is defined by bold color, directional lighting, and subjects who look exactly like themselves.

Based in Apopka, FL. Available for studio sessions, events, and on-location work throughout Central Florida.

"The goal is always the same — you leave looking like the best version of you."
