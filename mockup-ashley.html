About

"Documenting what Floridians see every day but never stop to look at."

Working across nature, portrait, surf, and commercial photography, Ashley Howard documents the overlooked corners of Florida's coast and landscape. Based in Port Orange, she brings macro precision to botanical subjects and cinematic patience to surf and candid portrait work. Her Roots series — close studies of Florida native plants from Jacksonville to Key West — treats the state's flora with the same attention usually reserved for gallery-bound fine art.
