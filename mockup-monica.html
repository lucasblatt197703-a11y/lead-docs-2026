About the Artist

Academic Tradition,
Living Work

Mónica Carmona is an oil painter and master instructor trained in the traditions of European academic ateliers. Holding a BFA from the Facultad de Artes y Diseño at UNAM, her portrait work is grounded in classical realism — careful observation, disciplined technique, and an attentiveness to the interior life of each subject.

"Academic training inspired by the traditions of European ateliers."

Based in Los Gatos, California, Carmona accepts portrait commissions and teaches oil painting and classical drawing to teens and adults through her atelier studio — one of the few programs in the Bay Area offering structured classical instruction from fundamentals through advanced portraiture.
