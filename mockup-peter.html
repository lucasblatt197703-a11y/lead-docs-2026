The Artist

Twenty years of painting Florida from the inside.

Peter Lagi is a Florida-based fine artist working in watercolor, acrylic, and airbrush. A Florida State University Studio Art graduate and lifelong Sunshine State native, Lagi's work is rooted in close observation of Florida's landscapes, wildlife, and light — from the wide Atlantic coastline of Volusia County to the quiet reaches of the St. Johns River basin.

"It's nice to meet other artists and see how they have their setup and do their artwork."

Working across three mediums — the transparency of watercolor, the depth of acrylic, and the precision of airbrush — Lagi brings technical range and a painter's eye to every commission and exhibition piece.
