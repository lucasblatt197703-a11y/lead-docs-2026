The Artist
"An artist is an artist through and through — trained or not, an artist was born to create."

Draycott is a self-taught mixed media artist based in Tampa, Florida — creating since childhood and now working primarily in digital painting and drawing. Her practice integrates photography with digital art, building layered compositions that bridge documentation and imagination.

Available for custom commissions, original works, and limited-edition prints. Each piece reflects a singular vision — color-driven, tactile, and entirely self-made.
