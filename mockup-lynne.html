The Artist

Lynne Raimondo

Working from her studio in Bonita Springs, Raimondo paints Florida's coastal world with a commitment to vivid color and captured light — seascapes, wetlands, egrets, palms, beach cottages, and the particular quality of Gulf air at dusk. Her work is sold through local galleries, Shangri-La Resort, Etsy, and direct from the studio.

"Vivid colors to capture beauty and light — that's what Southwest Florida gives you, if you slow down enough to look."
