About

Capturing connection,
not just moments.

Annabella Mowry is a Tampa-based photographer drawn to the unguarded moments — a quiet laugh between ceremony and reception, the way morning light falls across a kitchen table. Her work began photographing marine life at a rescue center in Clearwater, FL, where she first understood what it means to truly see something. Available in Tampa, FL and Tuscaloosa, AL for weddings, family sessions, elopements, and beyond.

"I started by photographing marine life. Now I photograph the people who matter most."

Every family has its own light.
