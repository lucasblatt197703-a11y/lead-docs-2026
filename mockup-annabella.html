02 — About

The Photographer

Annabella Mowry is a Tampa-based photographer whose career began photographing marine wildlife at the Clearwater aquatic rescue — training her eye for fleeting, unrepeatable moments in natural light.

That instinct now serves families, couples, and graduates throughout the Tampa Bay area. Annabella brings the same patience and precision to every session, whether it's a beach elopement at golden hour or a child's milestone moment.

Her approach is quiet and unobtrusive — she disappears so you can be present, and then you have the images to prove it happened.
