About the Artist

"Painting what you see,
not what you know."

Working in oil, acrylic, and pastel, Pintar's landscapes carry the warmth of the field — painted in direct observation across Florida, Wisconsin, and the American West. A former technical illustrator turned full-time painter, his work has been recognized at the Winter Park Paint Out, Mount Dora Plein Air, and Wisconsin State Fair, and is exhibited through Steidel Contemporary Gallery.

Based in The Villages, FL, his paintings are held in private collections and in the permanent public collection of Okahumpka Park.
