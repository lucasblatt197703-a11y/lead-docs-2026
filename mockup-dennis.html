About

Where sculpture becomes a mirror for the world around it.

Dennis Carbee creates mirror-polished stainless steel sculptures that do something most art cannot: they absorb their environment and reflect it back transformed. Every piece changes with the light, the season, and the viewer's position.

Based in Fort Lauderdale, Dennis accepts commissions for residential, hospitality, and corporate installations throughout South Florida and beyond. His pieces have found homes in luxury hotels, private estates, and public spaces.
