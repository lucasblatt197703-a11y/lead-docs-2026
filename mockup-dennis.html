About

Where sculpture becomes a mirror for the world around it.

Working exclusively in mirror-polished stainless steel for over three decades, Dennis Carbee has developed a singular body of work he calls Interactive Stainless Imagery™ — sculptures that transform their environment into something viewers have never seen before.

Based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, Carbee accepts commissions for residential, hospitality, and corporate installations throughout South Florida and internationally. Each piece is custom-fabricated to the space and the client.

His work has been installed in luxury hotels, private estates, corporate headquarters, and public spaces across three continents.
