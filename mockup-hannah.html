About

Hannah Holland is a documentary and fine-art photographer working across landscape, nature, and portraiture. Based between Green Cove Springs, Florida and the mountains of Western North Carolina, her work spans cinematic panoramas to intimate wedding coverage.

Trained at the University of North Florida and with four years of professional experience at Gumps Sports Photography, Holland brings technical discipline to subjects that reward patience — mountain light, forest stillness, and the quiet moments between ceremony and celebration.
