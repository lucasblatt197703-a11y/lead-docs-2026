The Work

Home of Some of Florida's Most Beautiful Venues

St. Augustine is different.

America's oldest city has a quality of light you won't find anywhere else in Florida — golden hour bounces off 400-year-old stone, Spanish moss filters everything into something cinematic. Dan has been working in these streets, courtyards, and chapels since 2000. He knows when the light falls right on the Castillo. He knows which garden at Lightner Museum goes golden at 5pm. This isn't a location — it's home.

About

Working from instinct rather than formula, Dan Florez has spent over two decades documenting the moments that actually matter — the ones that happen between the posed shots. Originally from North Carolina's Outer Banks, he earned his Fine Arts degree from Flagler College and has been based in St. Augustine since 2000.

"I've been photographing weddings in St. Augustine since 2007. I know these venues, I know this light."

Specializing in weddings, musical artists, and events across Northeast Florida, he brings the sensibility of a documentary filmmaker to every shoot — available to travel throughout Florida and beyond.
