FAQ

Common
Questions

When should I book?
As soon as possible — ideally in your first or second trimester. Birth photographers book by due date windows, and multiple clients can share nearby dates. The earlier you book, the more secure your spot.
What if my birth is really fast — or really long?
No time limits. Whether your labor is 2 hours or 20, Amanda stays with you from whenever you call until after golden hour with your new baby. You pay for the experience, not the hours.
Do you photograph hospital, birth center, and home births?
Yes — all three. Having delivered in hospital, birth center, and home settings herself, Amanda is comfortable and experienced in every birth environment.
What areas do you serve?
Primarily the Tampa Bay area including Land O' Lakes, Wesley Chapel, New Tampa, Brandon, St. Petersburg, and surrounding communities. Virtual doula services are available nationwide.
