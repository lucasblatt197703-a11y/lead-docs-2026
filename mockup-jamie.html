Jamie Roxx

Working in his signature Pop Noir style since 2001, Jamie Roxx creates bold, graphic portraits of cultural icons — celebrities, rock stars, and movie legends rendered in acrylic and oil on gallery-wrapped canvas. Based in Deerfield Beach, FL, his work has appeared in TV commercials, on CD covers for rock bands, and in private collections worldwide. He also hosts Pop Roxx Radio, a weekly podcast now at over 1,400 episodes.

"Every painting is completely hand-painted. Not a print. Not a reproduction."
