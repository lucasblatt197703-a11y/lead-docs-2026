02 — The Artist

Jamie Roxx

"Pop art isn't decoration. It's a conversation with culture — I just paint my end of it."

South Florida-based painter Jamie Roxx has been creating original pop art since 2001. Working across celebrity portraits, rock icons, and the visual language of American pop culture, Roxx produces over 300 original paintings a year — a volume that reflects obsession, not hustle.

The Scarlet Fever series — bold, high-contrast portraits of cultural figures — established Roxx as a voice in Florida's contemporary art scene. The work is immediate, unapologetic, and designed to stop you mid-step.

Commissions are open for custom portraits of any subject. Originals and prints are available. Based in Deerfield Beach, FL.
