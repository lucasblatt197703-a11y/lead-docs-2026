The Artist

"Painting what Florida actually looks like — not a postcard version of it."

Working in acrylic on canvas, Russ Taylor captures two deeply personal worlds: the rolling hills and wetlands of Central Florida's Pasco County, and the loose, joyful energy of blues, jazz, and bluegrass music. His landscapes favour loose realism — light filtered through live oak canopies, still water reflecting sky — while his music paintings embrace colour and whimsy.

Taylor relocated to the west side of Florida in 2015 and in 2018 opened Out of Our Hands Gallery in historic downtown Dade City alongside his wife Lee, herself a painter. The gallery represents over 25 Florida artists working across painting, glass, ceramics, printmaking, and metalwork.
