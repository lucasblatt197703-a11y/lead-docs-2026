06 — About

The Photographer

LaDora Sims is a wildlife and nature photographer based on Florida's Space Coast. Working primarily in the wetlands, refuges, and coastal habitats of Central Florida, she has built a body of work that documents the region's extraordinary biodiversity with patience and precision.

Her photographs are sold as fine art prints at shows across Florida, from Cedar Key to St Augustine Beach.

Her subjects — roseate spoonbills in sunrise light, manatees beneath clear springs, great blue herons mid-dance — are caught at moments most photographers never witness. Each image in her catalog represents hours in the field.
