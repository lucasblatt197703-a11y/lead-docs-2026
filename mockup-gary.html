05 — The Photographer
Gary Lester

Born and raised in West Palm Beach, Gary Lester has spent the past decade using photography as a vehicle for exploration — shooting in over 70 countries while building one of South Florida's most trusted names in wedding and portrait photography.

Armed with a Canon 6D, 24-105mm and 70-200mm MkII, Gary brings the compositional instincts of a travel photojournalist to every wedding, maternity session, and event he covers.

"Capturing the moments you'll never want to forget."

When he's not behind the lens, Gary is surfing the Florida coast, exploring the outdoors, and spending time with his family — the same values he brings to every client relationship.
