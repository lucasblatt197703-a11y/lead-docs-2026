Artist Statement
"I try to give it my very best. Art has been my life."
— Richard Davis, muralist since 1985

Working across acrylic, oil, airbrush, pencil, and colored pencil, Davis brings meticulous realism to every scale — from 9" pet portraits to 20-foot church canvases. His clients have included the U.S. Air Force, McDonald's, Texas Instruments, and the American Dental Association.

About the Artist

Three Decades.
Every Medium.

Richard Davis began as a sign painter and grew into one of Florida's most versatile muralists and illustrators. Over 30 years of professional work, his range has expanded to encompass fine art, military aviation paintings, book illustration, western art, and pet portraiture — all rendered with the same exacting attention to detail and realism.

Inspired early on by Norman Rockwell, Davis channels personal history — including a lifelong connection to aviation — into work that bridges the emotional and the technical. Based in Spring Hill, FL, he remains actively available for mural, illustration, and fine art commissions.
