About Katelyn

Katelyn Michelle is a Central Florida photographer specializing in the quiet, unhurried moments of early life. Based in Clermont, she works with families across the greater Orlando area, bringing warmth and patience to every newborn, maternity, and family session. She also volunteers with Preemie Prints, providing NICU families with photographs of their earliest days together.
