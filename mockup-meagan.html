About the Photographer

Capturing the moments that matter most.

Meagan Leffel has spent over a decade documenting the smallest, most irreplaceable moments — first breaths, new families, and the quiet tenderness of early parenthood. Based in Eagletown, Oklahoma, she specializes in newborn, family, and maternity photography, bringing genuine warmth and patience to every session.

Her goal is not simply to take photographs, but to create art worthy of your walls — images that become heirlooms passed from one generation to the next.

"My goal is not only to capture a beautiful picture — but to create art for your home."
