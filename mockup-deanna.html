About

"I was born with this gift. I just grew into it."

Working in oil and acrylic on canvas, Deanna Wheeler's paintings explore the life and character of animals, people, and the North Florida landscape surrounding her Pinetta studio. Her work has been exhibited at the Annette Howell Turner Center for the Arts in Valdosta, Georgia, and placed in private and public collections throughout the region. A self-educated artist who came to painting after a long career in nursing, Wheeler has devoted her retirement to the work she describes as her calling.
