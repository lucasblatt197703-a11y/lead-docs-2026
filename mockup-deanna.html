02 — The Artist

Deanna Wheeler

Working primarily in oil and acrylic on canvas, Deanna Wheeler creates portraits of animals, people, and the landscapes of North Florida that shaped her eye. Based in Pinetta, she paints from life and from a decades-long practice rooted in careful observation.

Over 15 years of exhibiting has brought her work to the Turner Center for the Arts and regional galleries throughout the Southeast. Her commissioned portrait for the State Attorney of Florida stands as a testament to the trust institutions place in her craft.

Commissions are open. Deanna works closely with clients to capture subjects — animal or human — with the depth and care they deserve.
