About

The Vision

"Displaying vibrant sounds and urban glamour for over 10 years."

MJ FLIX is a Miami-based photographer and visual director known for bold compositions, high-contrast storytelling, and a signature style that fuses raw energy with cinematic glamour. With a decade behind the lens spanning fashion, music, events, cosplay, and noir — MJ's work has appeared in WorldstarHipHop, Dimepiece Magazine, and HipHop Weekly. Named Official Curvehouse Magazine Photographer and Local Photographer of the Year, MJ brings an editorial eye to every frame.
