About
Peahen
Photography

Peahen Photography was built around one idea: that your real moments deserve more than a posed smile. Brooke Peacock has been photographing Treasure Coast families, couples, and newborns with a style that is warm, candid, and unmistakably Florida.

From golden-hour elopements on the coast to newborn sessions at home — every gallery tells a story worth keeping. All photos are included with every session, with unlimited print rights.

Serving Indian River, St. Lucie, Okeechobee, Martin, and Palm Beach counties. Travel available throughout South Florida.
