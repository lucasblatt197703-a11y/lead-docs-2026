About

Bianca Roman-Stumpff paints the creatures that live just outside of ordinary — the axolotl that reads your books, the wolf made of golden light, the moth that's a little too theatrical about it. Working in acrylic on panel and canvas, her work finds the cozy and the uncanny in equal measure.

Based in Orlando, she has spent the last eight years painting live at Universal Studios, Cafe Tutu Tango, and conventions across the country. Her Patreon community follows the work as it happens.

"I paint the creatures that live just outside of ordinary."
