About Bianca

Based in Orlando, FL, Bianca Roman-Stumpff paints worlds you want to step into: fantastical figures, luminous creatures, and dreamscapes that pull from mythology, folklore, and the quietly magical corners of everyday life. Working primarily in acrylic on canvas, her paintings have found their way into homes and collections across the world through a devoted community of collectors, Patreon supporters, and print buyers.
