About

"The world is more interesting with a little magic in it."

Bianca Roman-Stumpff is an acrylic painter based in Orlando, Florida, whose work explores whimsical fantasy through vibrant, detailed creatures — cryptids, dragons, axolotls, and mythological beings brought to life with warmth and wit.

Her work has been shown at art shows and conventions across Florida. Originals, prints, and commissions available.
