Fluffy Eagle

Oil on Canvas  ·  Figurative Series

Part of Nathan's ongoing mask series — figures drawn from Haitian ancestral traditions, rendered in saturated oil with geometric precision. The animal mask is not a disguise but a declaration: of origin, lineage, and chosen identity. Each painting in the series operates as both portraiture and ceremony.
