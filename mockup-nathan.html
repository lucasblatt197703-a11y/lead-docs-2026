02 — About

Rooted in Miami.
Made for Walls.

Nathan Delinois — known as Nate Dee — is a Miami-based painter and muralist whose figurative work bridges intimate oil portraiture and monumental public art. A two-time FIU alumnus, his work has appeared in the New York Times, on walls throughout Wynwood and Allapattah, and in commissions for Fortune 500 companies and the Miami Heat.

His ongoing Mask Series explores cultural identity, folklore, and transformation — rendered in rich, layered oils that reward close looking.

"I want people to see themselves in the work — whether it's a ten-inch painting or a ten-story wall."
