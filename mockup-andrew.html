About the Artist

Painter, Biologist,
Wildlife Enthusiast

Tilsley's practice is rooted in a lifelong fascination with the natural world — one that began in childhood and was formally deepened through studies in both art and biology. A conservationist at heart, he has contributed field work with sea turtles in Greece and Costa Rica, bringing that empirical intimacy directly into the studio.

"His inspiration comes from anger and beauty — a rare combination that makes every piece impossible to ignore."

Paintings and limited-edition prints are available. Originals span birds, mammals, and reptiles. Conservation themes run throughout.
