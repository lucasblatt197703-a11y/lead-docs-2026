About

Grant-Valkaria, Brevard County

"Every frame is a document of something real."

Working across weddings, commercial, and documentary work, Laura Marie Pepsin has spent 15 years photographing Florida's coastline and the people who call it home. Based near Sebastian Inlet in Grant-Valkaria, Brevard County, her work is known for its warmth, its light, and its patience — portraits that feel like memory rather than performance.

With a background in photojournalism and commercial work, every session is approached with the same care: unhurried, observant, and rooted in the particular beauty of East Central Florida.
