About the Artist

Keri Keene

A Florida native with roots in both the panhandle and the Treasure Coast, Keene draws from the landscapes and animals she has lived among her entire life. Trained early by her mother, fellow pastelist Melody May Keene, she developed a rigorous hand-drawing practice across pastel, graphite, and color pencil. Her work — animals, pet portraits, and Florida landscapes — has been shown at the Vero Beach Main Street Gallery and earned her membership in the Village Arts Framing & Gallery in Ponte Vedra Beach.

"I take time and care with each portrait, building the drawing in layers to capture not just accuracy, but also the familiar expression that makes your pet unique."
