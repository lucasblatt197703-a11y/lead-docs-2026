"Every face holds a story — I paint the feeling beneath the surface."

Working in watercolor, Auretha Callison creates portraits that go beyond likeness — warm, textured works that radiate the humanity of each subject. Based in St. Augustine, Florida, she accepts commissions for portraits of loved ones, pets, and custom compositions, with each piece hand-painted on archival paper.
