About

Working primarily in watercolor, Auretha Callison's portraits and landscapes capture the quality of light that makes a moment feel inhabited. Based in St. Augustine, FL, she has been painting since childhood and offers commissions for portraits, pets, landscapes, and personal subjects.

She is also an Intuitive Stylist — helping clients align their outer presence with who they truly are.

"The light in watercolor is like nothing else — you don't paint it, you reveal it."
