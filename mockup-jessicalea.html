About Jessica

Burlington, NC photographer specializing in life's biggest moments.

Based in Burlington, North Carolina, Jessica Lea has spent more than a decade documenting weddings, newborns, families, maternity, seniors, and dancers. Her approach is direct and warm — get in close, go easy on the posing, and let real people be real people. She travels regularly to NC beaches, Virginia Beach, and Houston for clients who want her specific eye.

"I want the photos to feel like you — not like a photo session."

The Ivy Collection — Jessica's daughter runs a complementary creative venture alongside the photography business, making this a genuinely family-built creative practice.
