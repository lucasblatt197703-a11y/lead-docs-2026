04 · The Artist

Preserving
Rural Florida

Working primarily in oil on canvas, Sharon Schindelwig documents the country farms, old roadways, and open fields of Central and Eastern Florida — the landscapes that construction and development are steadily erasing.

Based in the Crestview area, she exhibits throughout Florida's art festival circuit, bringing her originals and prints directly to collectors who understand what's being lost.

"My art aims to preserve one beautiful day and one particular view that may soon be just a memory."
